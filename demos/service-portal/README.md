# Service Business Job Portal (Demo)

Portfolio demo for **Support Side** - a client-facing job portal for a fictional HVAC and plumbing contractor (**Acme Home Services**).

## What it demonstrates

- Two roles: **Staff** (sees all clients/jobs, updates status) and **Client** (sees their own jobs only)
- Job list with status badges, priority, and type
- Job detail with full status history timeline and documents list
- Staff can update job status and add notes (visible to client)
- New service request form (client or staff)
- Clients dashboard
- All data persisted in the browser (`localStorage`)
